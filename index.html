About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led institute focused on addressing critical digital, cyber, and AI risks in high-consequence, regulated environments such as financial services and critical national infrastructure.

We combine cutting-edge academic research from leading UK universities with real-world deployment through industry projects. Our mission is to close the skills gap by training and deploying the next generation of risk professionals who can navigate complex technological threats with confidence and expertise.
